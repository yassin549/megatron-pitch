Revenue Model

A phased monetization strategy scaling with our ecosystem

Current (2025)

Transaction Fees

Our core revenue comes from a standard 0.05% fee on every trade executed on the platform. As we bootstrap liquidity and grow our user base, this volume-based model provides immediate, scalable cash flow directly tied to platform activity.

Growth Phase (2026‚Äì2027)

Institutional Data & APIs

As Megatron becomes the standard for measuring real-world trends, we will introduce tiered SaaS subscriptions for institutional clients. Hedge funds and algorithmic traders will pay for high-frequency, low-latency API access to our unique index data and sentiment scores.

Expansion (2028+)

Marketplace & Listing Fees

We will evolve into an open marketplace where third-party creators can launch their own trend indices. We will generate revenue through asset listing fees and revenue-share agreements on the trading volume of these community-created markets.
